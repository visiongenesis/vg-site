Objections

Plain answers before you take the first call.

We already tried AI tools. Why would this be different?

Most failed AI attempts start with a tool. We start with the workflow. The tool only matters after we know the bottleneck, the owner of the process, the approval points, the data, and the business case.

What does this actually cost?

Focused Pain-Point Pilots typically run $8K-$25K for one workflow over four to eight weeks. Larger advisory or managed implementations are scoped after the audit, when the work is concrete.

Do I need a technical team for this to work?

No. We work inside the tools and constraints you already have wherever possible. Your team needs to know the work. We bring the AI build capacity, documentation, training, and support.

How long until we see results?

The first useful result should come during the pilot, not six months later. The goal is one installed workflow in four to eight weeks, followed by training and 30 days of monitoring.

How is this different from us just using ChatGPT ourselves?

ChatGPT can help an individual move faster. We install repeatable workflows inside the business, with data, handoffs, approvals, training, and a playbook the team owns.
