The problem

Event packages are great for revenue. They're terrible for tracking.

Your Glammiversary and Botox & Bingo events move real volume — but between the night of the sale and the last treatment delivery months later, the tracking gap creates operational drag: spreadsheets get stale, staff can't answer "what do I have left?", unredeemed units sit in a blind spot, and provider bonuses take hours to reconcile.

This tool eliminates that gap. It tracks every package from point of sale through every redemption, computes provider bonuses automatically, and gives your office manager a print-ready daily ledger.

How it works

From event night to treatment chair

Event night

Packages are entered at point of sale. Customer, items, payment method, and event are recorded. The system starts tracking from this moment.

Treatment delivery

As patients come in for their treatments, staff logs each redemption — who delivered it, what was used, how many units. The outstanding balance updates in real time.

Ongoing visibility

At any point, your office manager can see who has unredeemed units, who's approaching deadline, and what the total outstanding liability looks like. No spreadsheet required.

Bonus reconciliation

Provider compensation is computed automatically from delivered treatments, per the rules you set. Print or export when it's time for payroll.
