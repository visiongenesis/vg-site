What's live in this demo
  • Order entry — replaces paper menus at the event itself. Mobile-first, all SKUs, live grand total.
  • Customer view — sorted by outstanding $ (the unallocated bucket made visible); deadline-aware reminder drafts.
  • Event P&L — revenue, customers, avg ticket, outstanding $, by-category, top SKUs.
  • Redemption flow — log a treatment against an outstanding package with provider attribution.
  • Bonus accruals — flat % per-provider per-category, computed from the redemption ledger.
  • EMR import — drop a CSV (or use the demo button), auto-map procedure codes to SKUs, propose redemptions for review.
