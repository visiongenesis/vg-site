Will Vision Genesis replace my staff?

No. We do not serve companies whose explicit goal is headcount reduction. AI multiplies your team's capacity. If your operating thesis is "smaller team," we are the wrong firm — and we'll tell you that in writing within 48 hours of intake.

Do I need to understand AI to work with you?

No. The operator on your engagement translates AI capability into operating decisions. Your job is to know your business; ours is to install the system that makes AI actually work inside it.

What do you actually install?

AI operating systems specific to how your business runs — research, drafting, scheduling, monitoring, reporting, follow-through, decision-support. The first artifact ships in two weeks. The full system is typically deployed in 60-90 days.

If we're the wrong firm, we'll tell you in writing within 48 hours of intake. If we engage and a particular system isn't delivering, we say so directly and refund the unused scope. Truth before sales pressure — that's Principle 02 of how the firm runs.

You do. The systems we install belong to you. The playbook is yours when we're done — even if we never work together again. We optionally retain you on a software subscription for ongoing operation, but that's your choice, not a lock-in.
